Customer Authorisation 客户授权

By signing this form, I authorise CovaU to: 通过签署本表格，我授权CovaU：

  1. Remotely access and control my Battery at any time during the VPP Services Term for the purposes of the VPP Program. 在VPP服务期间随时远程访问和控制我的电池，以用于VPP计划的目的。
  2. Access my Supply Address for on-site work by CovaU technicians or the installer. 访问我的供电地址，以便CovaU技术人员或安装人员进行现场工作。
  3. Disclose my Data to third parties that are directly involved in administering the VPP Program, including CovaU's VPP Platform provider or technology partner, for purposes such as Battery status and may need my Supply Address or an identifier for site coordination. 向直接参与管理VPP计划的第三方披露我的数据，包括CovaU的VPP平台提供商或技术合作伙伴，用于电池状态等目的，可能需要我的供电地址或站点协调标识符。
  4. Complete the battery connection. 完成电池连接。
  5. Engaged the installer to complete the connection, if I requested the battery installation. 如果我要求电池安装，则聘请安装人员完成连接。
